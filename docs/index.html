How It Works

1

Choose a Shape

Pick from six tactile shapes: stamp, circle, ticket, label, torn paper, or rectangle.

2

Snap or Import

Take a photo with the shape overlay, or import from your library.

3

Automatically Cut

Snipbook masks your photo to the shape instantly. No cropping needed.

4

Keep Forever

Your snip is added to your book. Watch your collection grow.
